AI for Business Decisions

Recent Work

Cyber Attacks

Analyze the cyber security attacks and provide AI-driven prevention strategies. Due to cyber attacks most organizations face issues that include, financial losses, reputation damage, and legal liabilities.

Lung Cancer Prediction

The goal of this project was to prioritize patients based on their risk of mortality, ensuring that high-risk patients receive timely and appropriate medical interventions. We used AI and ML algorithms to help us identify patterns and develop a predictive model for improved preventive measures

Flight Price Prediction

Predict domestic flight prices in India and help airlines optimize revenue, enhance customer satisfaction, and improve operational efficiency. By predicting flight prices, airlines can make informed decisions regarding dynamic pricing, route, schedule optimization, and class configuration

Loblaw

Focused on predicting customer churn for Loblaws Digital, an e-commerce platform that offers groceries, merchandise, and financial services. The goal was to identify customers at risk of leaving and help the company develop targeted retention strategies.

TikTok

TikTok is a Chinese social media app that allows users to create, watch, and share short videos. Due to its interactive content, it has gained popularity globally, mainly among young audiences. It uses unique strategies like TikTok trends and challenges to keep the users engaged. For this project, we performed analysis on user account data and tiktok app reviews.

GoodLife

GoodLife Fitness is a leading fitness club chain in Canada, that is well known for its wide range of group fitness classes and gym facilities. With a commitment to promoting health and wellness, GoodLife provides its members with access to a wide variety of fitness programs, advanced workout equipment, and personalized training services. In this project, we focused to optimize gym classes and equipment utilization
